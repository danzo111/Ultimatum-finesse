Ultimatum Finesse is a proudly citizen-owned, Gaborone-based geospatial firm built around one idea: a map is only useful if the people using it can trust every point on it.

Our Story

A multidisciplinary geospatial consultancy, built in Gaborone

Ultimatum Finesse (Pty) Ltd was established in 2013 to provide professional, innovative and technically sound services across the geospatial environment — drone services, surveying, mining, construction and BIM. We are certified by the Civil Aviation Authority of Botswana (CAAB) for commercial drone operations.

Our Vision: To create a more informed and sustainable future where geospatial insights are seamlessly integrated into decision-making across diverse industries.

Our Mission: To be the leading innovator in geospatial solutions, empowering our clients to make informed decisions through the power of GIS, advanced surveying techniques, and drone technology.

Today, our six service disciplines work together: a single flight can feed a GIS layer, a BIM model and a volumetric survey at the same time.
