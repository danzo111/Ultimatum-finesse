Water & Energy Infrastructure

Geoflux

Our largest programme of joint work: QA/QC surveying during the Kanye water network emergency works (2023–2024), the 754 km Phokojwe–Kazungula powerline cadastral survey, 100+ km of pipeline corridor topographic surveys at Kanye and Moshupa, and reservoir bottom-level surveys.
